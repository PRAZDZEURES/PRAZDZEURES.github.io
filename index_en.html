The shelter

A cabin of stone and weathered wood, in an amphitheater of greenery suspended at 1750m altitude, with a view of all the Aravis, such is the Praz Dzeures shelter. His particuliarity ? to be managed by goatherds who reconcile work in the pasture and public reception to share in this idyllic environment, the time of a meal or an evening, the way of life which is theirs.

Located on the eastern side of the massif of Tournette in Haute Savoie, the Praz Dzeures shelter belongs to the municipality of Serraval. It has kept its ancient name meaning prairie (Praz) located at two o'clock (Dzeures) walk from the nearest village. Indeed, it is the average walking time it takes for hikers to reach the refuge from the parking lot of La Molloire.
In Winter the cottage is completely buried under the snow.

Activities

Hiking

The cottage facing the Mont Blanc overlooks the mountain of Sulens and the village of Serraval. It contemplates in its entirety the chain of Aravis, from Mount Charvin to Point PercÃ©e.
Hikers, after an hour of walking towards the tip of the Bajulaz, can admire a splendid panorama with Lake Annecy at their feet then further Lake Geneva and the Bauges and Jura mountains.
From the tip of the Bajulaz, the climb to the top of the Tournette is possible. The Tournette, whose summit rises to 2351m, overlooks Lake Annecy and the surrounding peaks and offers a magnificent panorama of the landscape below and the surrounding mountains. In the shape of an armchair, the Tournette is THE mountain to climb. Holidaymakers rub shoulders in summer, the Savoyards climb in all seasons with walking shoes in summer and snowshoes in winter. Be careful however this route is somewhat technical in its final part: indeed, many passages are equipped with cables and chains. The final ascent is possible thanks to the presence of two metal ladders. Approximate walking time from the refuge Praz Dzeures about 2h.
